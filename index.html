Background

From failing and leaving UC Riverside his first year out of high school Francisco had to make a decision whether to pursue university or give up and work. Francisco declared he was not done and was not going to give up that easily. He enrolled into Los Angeles mission college and with no idea on what major to pursue he chose to take a computer science course. From there Francisco built the love and passion to create and build projects that can help make the world easier. After completing rigorous course work he was able to finish the necessary requirements to recieve an Associates degree for computer science and Mathmatics. Francisco was able to transfer to California State University Northridge within the year of 2020. When Francisco transfered, the pandemic began but that did not stop him from completing his dream to become a graduate. After 2 years of hard work and online courses he was able to accomplish his dream and graduated from his University with the degree for Computer Science. Apart from battling course work Francisco spent most of his extra time powerlifting and competing within the sport , he was able to build relationships with others. Francisco lives within Los Angeles, California and is looking to find work within Software engineering or development within his area or to work remotely to pursue his desire to learn more.
